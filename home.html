About

This online journal is aimed to provide a fruitful interaction between political philosohpy and social sciences and to forge a critical approach to our society in this troubled situation, in which democracy is backsliding in many parts of the world and nonetheless is widely taken for granted. We want to enrich the public debate and to enhance the public awareness about some crucial topics in the domain of a democratic society: such as the conditions through which rights are gained and their practicability in the context of a complex world.
